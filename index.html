Toasted Penne alla Vodka

    Ingredients
  • 4 cups....Chicken or Vegetable stock
  • 2 tbsp....olive oil
  • 2 tbsp....butter
  • 1 lbs....penne rigate
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 4....garlic cloves, crushed or grated
  • ....salt
  • 1/2 tsp....crushed red pepper
  • 1....fresh bay leaf
  • 1 cup....vodka
  • 1 pt. (2 cups)....cherry tomatoes,halved
  • 2 cups....passata or tomato puree
  • 1/3 cup....freshly grated parmigiano-reggiano, plus more for serving
    Directions
  1. In a medium saucepan, bring stock to a boil, then reduce heat to low and keep warm
  2. In a large, deep skillet, heat the oil over medium high. Add the butter and let it melt into the oil. Add the pasta; cook, stirring often, until golden and fragrant (about 2-4 minutes).
  3. Add the onion and garlic and cook; stirring often, until softened (about 2-4 minutes).
  4. Season the pasta with the salt and crushed red pepper, and add the bay leaf. Add the vodka and cook, stirring constantly, until fully absorbed (about 1 minute).
  5. Add 1 1/2 to 2 cups stock (a few ladlefuls), the cherry tomoatoes, and half the passata; stir until the liquid is greatly reduced, but not dry (about 5-6 minutes). Add the remaining passata and basil.
  6. cook, stirring often, until the sauce is reduced and the pasta is al dente, adding water by the 1/4 cupful if the sauce is reduced before the pasta is cooked (about 10 minutes more).
  7. Stir in about 1c cheese, serve pasta in shallow bowls. Pass more cheese at the table for serving.
